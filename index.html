How It Works

Care reimagined — in three steps

Qluve integrates seamlessly into your care home's daily routine without disrupting your team's workflow.

1

Qluve Listens & Connects

Residents speak naturally with Qluve's voice AI — sharing feelings, stories, and concerns. Sentiment analysis monitors emotional wellbeing continuously, flagging changes before they become problems.

2

Smart Reminders & Support

Qluve gently reminds residents about medications, meals, and activities — reducing missed doses and boosting engagement. Family members can send voice messages directly to their loved ones through the platform.

3

Caregivers Stay Informed

A real-time dashboard surfaces mood trends, wellbeing alerts, and two-way messaging so care managers can act quickly and document efficiently — saving hours each week per resident.

Growth Roadmap

Built for the future of Nordic care

From Finland's care homes to across the Nordics — Qluve's roadmap is designed for scale, depth, and clinical impact.

🇫🇮

Nordic Care Home Expansion Playbook

Target Finland's 900+ municipal care facilities and private chains (Attendo, Esperi, Mehiläinen) with a structured pilot-to-deployment program. Use existing resident testimonials and ViisSeiska/Myllykaari case studies to build trust. Aim for 50+ facilities within 12 months by aligning with municipal procurement cycles and demonstrating cost savings in caregiver time.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Family Connection Portal

Launch a family-facing mobile app where relatives can view mood trend summaries, receive wellbeing alerts, and send voice messages to residents through Qluve. This creates emotional value for families, gives care homes a differentiation selling point, and opens a potential B2C subscription revenue stream alongside the core B2B model.

📊

Predictive Wellbeing Analytics

Leverage the longitudinal mood and conversation data Qluve already collects to build predictive models that detect early signs of cognitive decline, depression, UTIs (through behavioral change), and social isolation trends. Package this as a premium analytics dashboard for care managers and sell it as a clinical decision-support upgrade.

🇸🇪

Sweden Market Entry

Expand into Sweden as the first international market — similar welfare model, large elderly care sector (85,000+ institutional care residents), and strong appetite for health tech innovation. Partner with a Swedish distributor or care chain like Ambea or Humana for a lighthouse pilot project. Leverage 50+ language support for immediate Swedish-language deployment.

🔗

Care System Integrations

Build API integrations with major Nordic care management platforms (Hilkka, Lifecare, TietoEVRY wellness) so Qluve data flows directly into existing documentation workflows. This reduces caregiver documentation burden, makes Qluve indispensable to daily operations, and dramatically increases switching costs once deployed.

🤖

Voice-Powered AI Conversations

Natural, empathetic conversations available 24/7 — reducing loneliness and isolation while gathering continuous wellbeing data. Qluve speaks the resident's language, remembers their preferences, and builds a genuine relationship over time.
